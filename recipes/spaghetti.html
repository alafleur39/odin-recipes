Spaghetti Recipe

We've all heard of spaghetti before. It is a classic italian fine cuisine. Enjoyed by everyone of all different backgrounds. Today our website will provide you a simple recipe of this famous dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. 1. Gather Ingredients: Spaghetti pasta Water Salt Olive oil (optional) Your choice of sauce (e.g., marinara, pesto, or alfredo) Parmesan cheese (optional)
  2. 2. Boil Water: Fill a large pot with water and add a generous amount of salt (about 1-2 tablespoons). Bring the water to a rolling boil over high heat.
  3. 3. Cook the Spaghetti: Add the spaghetti to the boiling water. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking. Cook according to the package instructions (usually around 8-12 minutes) until al dente.
  4. 4. Drain the Pasta: Once cooked, turn off the heat and carefully drain the spaghetti in a colander. (You can reserve some pasta water if you want to adjust the sauce later.)
  5. 6. Add Sauce: Return the spaghetti to the pot or place it in a serving bowl. Add your chosen sauce and mix well. If the sauce is too thick, add a little reserved pasta water to achieve the desired consistency.
  6. 7. Serve: Plate the spaghetti and garnish with olive oil and Parmesan cheese, if desired.
  7. 8.Enjoy!: Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade spaghetti!
